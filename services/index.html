Frequently asked questions

What is the SOP triple-duty mechanic?

We take each SOP and turn it into three things from one source. First, a training course with AI-generated video, quizzes, and certification. Second, an automation that runs the task itself in n8n, Zapier, or Power Automate. Third, an AOP, a procedure your AI agent can run. One SOP becomes how people learn the job, how the routine work gets done, and how an agent can operate it.

Do we have to buy all five services at once?

No. Most clients start with AI enablement and training, see it work, and grow into the training system, automations, coaching, and the care plan over time. The offers are a journey, not a fixed package.

What tools do you build automations on?

We build on n8n, Zapier, and Power Automate, depending on what fits your stack. QuarterSmart has 25 published automation templates in the n8n public library with more than 10,000 total uses.

What happens after the system is built?

Every engagement ends at a recurring baseline, not at zero. Our care plan hosts and maintains your training system and automations monthly, keeps integrations running, and updates courses as your processes change.
