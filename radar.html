What is QuarterSmart Signals?

QuarterSmart Signals is a curated AI insight hub published by QuarterSmart, an AI implementation and workforce enablement partner. Each post covers a recent development in AI — a new model, a tool change, a pricing shift, or a policy move — and translates it into plain language for operators and team leaders. The goal is not to chase every headline, but to flag the signals that affect how organizations actually assess, train, pilot, automate, and scale AI. QuarterSmart is founded by Hyrum Hurst, a verified n8n creator with 25 public workflow templates.
