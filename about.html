At Learning Journey AI, Hyrum builds and maintains the company platform and website and develops its course creation workflows, and the team has delivered hands-on AI coding workshops at events including the SCSP AI+ Expo in Washington DC.

For Structure Properties, a San Francisco property management firm overseeing 4,000+ units, he built Quackfolio, a custom white label onboarding system spanning seven roles, with 39 training videos, Claude integrated quiz gating, automated certification, and compliance tracking. He also works pro bono with Ability Builders, a Mesa, Arizona nonprofit that helps people with disabilities find and keep employment, bringing AI workflows into their daily operations.

Certifications: Google Certified AI Automations Engineer, Google Generative AI Leader, Google Prompt Engineering, Google Data Analytics, and the Vanderbilt AI Automation Certification.

What QuarterSmart actually is

QuarterSmart was not built to sell an empty platform or a one off workshop. It helps organizations find where AI pays off, train the people who use it, ship working systems, and measure what changes. When a workflow needs custom build work, QuarterSmart ships it. The point is operational adoption: repeatable, team wide usage that changes how work gets done.

The work runs across three connected service areas: AI implementation, workforce enablement, and n8n automation. QuarterSmart is based in Mesa, Arizona and works with organizations across Arizona and the United States.
