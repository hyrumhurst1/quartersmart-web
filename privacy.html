Last updated: April 11, 2026

AI Ed ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by QuarterSmart. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit aied.dev.

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide:

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

3. Data Storage and Security

Your email is transmitted securely and stored in our internal systems. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the site:

5. Cookies

AI Ed does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not use analytics services like Google Analytics.

6. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • View the data we have about you
  • Delete your data from our systems
  • Unsubscribe from all communications

To exercise these rights, email privacy@aied.dev.

7. Children's Privacy

AI Ed is designed for professionals and adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our platform evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
