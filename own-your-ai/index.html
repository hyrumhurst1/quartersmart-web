The trap

Renting intelligence forever has a cost

AI is becoming a fixed line item, like rent, that most businesses will pay indefinitely with no asset to show for it. The risk is not just the bill.

  • The bill never ends. Routine AI work runs through a metered API every month, and you own none of it.
  • Access can be gated or deprecated. The model you build on today may be restricted, retired, or regulated tomorrow.
  • The vendor sets the price. Once your work depends on an API, the vendor has pricing power, and AI hardware keeps trending more expensive.
  • Your data leaves the building, and the limits bite. Sensitive work runs on someone else's servers, and you hit usage caps and session limits at the worst moments.
The idea

An offset, not a replacement

You do not replace AI. You offset most of it onto something you own, the way solar offsets your power bill without taking you off the grid. Route the routine majority of requests (roughly 80 percent) to a private system in your building, and still call a frontier model for the hard 20 percent.

Cut the recurring bill

Move the routine bulk of your AI work off the metered API and onto hardware you own.

Keep your data in-house

Sensitive work runs on a box you control, in your building, not on a vendor's servers.

QuarterSmart does not make hardware. This is a system you own, built on proven hardware, with our software and your data. Throughput is bounded by your own box, so there are no vendor-imposed limits, but a bigger workload still wants a bigger machine.
