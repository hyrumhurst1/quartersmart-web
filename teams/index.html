How It Works

What does an AI rollout actually involve?

An AI rollout is the work of turning a few AI experiments into org-wide, measurable adoption. QuarterSmart follows a six-stage method — assess, train, pilot, automate, scale, and measure. We start by mapping your real workflows, build fluency and internal champions, pilot the highest-friction work, automate it on the tools you already use, scale what proves out, and measure adoption against a baseline so leaders can see what changed.

1

Assess

We map how your team actually works, where the friction is, and which AI opportunities are worth the effort. You leave with a prioritized rollout plan and timeline.

2

Train & Pilot

We build role-specific fluency and internal champions, then pilot a high-friction workflow with the people who own it — proving value before anything scales.

3

Automate

We ship working automations built on n8n and your approved models, integrated into the tools your team already uses, with SOPs so the team can run them.

4

Scale & Measure

We measure adoption and impact against the baseline, scale the workflows that prove out, and keep iterating so the gains hold across the team.
