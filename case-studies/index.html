Frequently asked questions

Can I talk to your clients before signing?

Yes. Once we scope a fit, we are happy to connect you with a reference. This client has approved us sharing their story, and we can walk you through exactly what we built and how it performs.

How do you measure results like the 65% and 36% numbers?

We baseline before we build. For this client that meant tracking onboarding time, repeat questions, and SOP-related mistakes across seven roles, then comparing against the same measures after the system went live.

My business looks nothing like property management. Does this still apply?

The mechanic is industry-agnostic. If your team runs on processes, those processes can become both training and automation. We have shipped for property management, an AI startup, and service businesses.

Tell us what your team does by hand today. We will show you how the same SOPs can teach it and run it.
