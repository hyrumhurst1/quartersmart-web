Client proof card pairing an outcome headline with measured stats.

```jsx
<CaseStudyCard
  client="Structure Properties"
  title="An AI training system that replaced rewriting SOPs by hand"
  summary="A 39-course video library across seven roles with Claude-powered quizzes and an owner dashboard."
  stats={[
    { value: "65%", label: "less onboarding time" },
    { value: "36%", label: "fewer SOP mistakes" },
  ]}
  tags={["Training", "Onboarding"]}
  href="/case-studies/structure-properties"
/>
```

Keep stats to two or three; they sit below a hairline divider. Numbers should be verifiable, never inflated.
