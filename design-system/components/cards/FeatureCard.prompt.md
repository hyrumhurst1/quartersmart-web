Service / feature card using a mono step number (matches the site's numbered offer ladder).

```jsx
<FeatureCard index="1" title="AI enablement and training" href="/services">
  We get your team comfortable using AI in their day to day work.
</FeatureCard>
```

Use `eyebrow` instead of `index` for non-sequential features. Setting `href` makes the whole card clickable and reveals the mono "Learn more →" affordance.
