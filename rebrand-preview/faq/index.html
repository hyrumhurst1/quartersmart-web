What does QuarterSmart do?
QuarterSmart is an AI implementation company that builds AI onboarding, training, and automation systems for growing teams. We train your team on AI, turn your standard operating procedures into training courses your people learn from, and build the long manual tasks into automations that run themselves. We then host and maintain what we build, so the system keeps working after launch. QuarterSmart is based in Mesa, Arizona and led by founders Hyrum Hurst, who builds the systems, and Cullen Brown, who manages engagements.
What is the SOP-to-training-and-automation mechanic?
QuarterSmart turns each standard operating procedure into two things from a single source. First, a training course your team learns from, built with AI-generated video, quizzes, and certification. Second, an automation that runs the task itself using tools like n8n, Zapier, or Power Automate. Same SOP, two outputs. One document becomes both how your people learn the job and how the work gets done without them.
What tools and platforms does QuarterSmart use?
QuarterSmart builds automations in n8n, Zapier, and Power Automate, and uses AI to generate training video, courses, and quizzes, including Claude-powered quiz generation. We deploy training and onboarding systems on platforms like Vercel and build custom applications with stacks such as Next.js, Cloudflare, and D1 when an engagement calls for it. Founder Hyrum Hurst is a Google Certified AI Automations Engineer and a verified top-50 n8n creator with public templates used more than 10,000 times.
Who does QuarterSmart work with?
QuarterSmart serves growing businesses, franchises, and teams that are heavy on operations, HR, and learning and development, especially high-turnover teams where onboarding never stops. We also work with nonprofits and education organizations. Separately, we partner with AI consultancies, agencies, and training firms as their white-label AI implementation partner, where they own the client and we build, document, and hand off the work.
How do engagements work?
A QuarterSmart engagement usually starts with AI enablement and training for your team. From there we turn your SOPs into a training system with AI-generated courses, quizzes, certification, and an owner reporting dashboard, then build your longest manual tasks into automations. Every engagement is designed to end at a recurring baseline of hosting, maintenance, and coaching, not at zero. To start, book a call and we will scope what to build first. Does QuarterSmart host and maintain what it builds?
Yes. QuarterSmart offers a Care Plan that hosts and maintains your training system and automations on a recurring monthly basis. We keep the courses current, watch the automations, and fix things when tools or processes change. This means every engagement ends at a recurring baseline where the system stays live and supported, rather than handing you something that quietly breaks after launch.
Does QuarterSmart offer coaching or ongoing consulting?
Yes. QuarterSmart runs recurring coaching and consulting calls, typically bi-weekly or monthly, where anyone on your team can hop on with questions, get help, and learn. When you need hands-on help to build something specific, we take that on as additional consulting. The goal is that your team keeps getting more capable with AI over time instead of depending on us for every change.
