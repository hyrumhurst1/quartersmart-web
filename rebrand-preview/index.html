The problem is not interest. The problem is the gap between learning AI and actually using it. Training that never reaches a real workflow is just a slideshow. The signature move: your SOPs do double duty

Write the SOP once. We turn it into two things that share the same source.

A training course your team learns from

AI-generated video walkthroughs, quizzes, and certification. New hires watch the real process instead of slogging through a document. Anyone can review any step in a click. Owners get a dashboard showing who has completed what and where people get stuck.

How an engagement works

We follow a simple path: land, build, automate, coach, retain. Every step builds on the last, and every engagement ends at a recurring baseline instead of dropping back to zero.

1. AI enablement and training

We start by training your team on AI. Low risk, fast value, and the easiest way to see what your operation actually needs. This is the door we walk in through.

2. SOPs to a training system

We turn your SOPs into AI-generated courses, quizzes, and certification, plus an owner dashboard that shows completion and trouble spots across every role.

3. SOPs to automations

The painful manual tasks get built into automations and bundled into the same system. The work that used to eat your team's day starts running on its own.

4. Coaching and consulting calls

Recurring bi-weekly or monthly calls where anyone on your team hops on for questions, help, and coaching. When something new comes up that needs building, we build it.

5. Hosting and maintenance

We host and maintain the training system and the automations so they keep working. You own a system that gets looked after, not a handoff you have to babysit.
