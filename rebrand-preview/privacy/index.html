Privacy policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

QuarterSmart keeps this simple, the same way we keep our work simple. This page explains what happens to information when you visit quartersmart.com or get in touch with us.

Who we are

QuarterSmart is an AI implementation company based in Mesa, Arizona. You can reach us at hyrum@quartersmart.com with any question about this policy or the information we hold.

What this site collects

This is a static marketing website. We do not run advertising cookies, we do not run analytics that profile you, and we do not ask you to create an account. We do not set tracking cookies of our own.

Information you send us

If you email us, including through the Book a call or Request a proposal links, we receive whatever you choose to share: your name, your email address, and what you tell us about your team. We use it only to reply to you and to scope possible work. We do not sell it, and we do not share it with anyone who is not helping us respond to you.

Fonts and other services

The site loads the Space Grotesk and JetBrains Mono typefaces from Google Fonts. When your browser requests those fonts, Google may receive your IP address and standard request information. We use no other third-party trackers. The site is served by our hosting provider, which may log standard server request data such as IP address and browser type for security and reliability.

Your choices

You can email hyrum@quartersmart.com at any time to ask what information we hold about you, to correct it, or to ask us to delete it. We will do our best to honor reasonable requests promptly.

Children

This site is meant for businesses and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site or our practices change. The date at the top shows the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about privacy go to hyrum@quartersmart.com.
