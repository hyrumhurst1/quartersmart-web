The signature mechanic: SOPs that do double duty

Here is what makes the work worth selling. We turn each client SOP into two things from one source.

  1. A training course the team learns from. AI-generated video, quizzes, and certification.
  2. An automation that runs the task itself. n8n, Zapier, or Power Automate.

Same SOP. Two outputs. One becomes how people learn the job. The other becomes how the work gets done without them. You can sell training and automation as one coherent system, not two separate projects you have to stitch together.

Frequently asked questions

Are you a subcontractor?
No. We work as your AI implementation partner. You own the client, the strategy, and the relationship. We build, test, document, and hand off the systems under your brand. Think of us as your delivery team for AI work, not a vendor the client ever sees.
Will my client know QuarterSmart is involved?
Only if you want them to. The work can stay fully white-label, with everything presented under your name. If you prefer, we can join calls as part of your team. You decide how visible we are.
What do you actually build?
AI training systems and automations. We turn client SOPs into AI-generated courses with quizzes and certification, and into automations that run the work in n8n, Zapier, or Power Automate. We also build owner reporting dashboards and can host and maintain everything on a recurring plan.
Do I need technical staff on my side?
No. That is the point. You handle strategy and the client relationship. We handle the build, the testing, and the documentation. You do not need to hire or manage engineers.
How do hand-offs work?
We deliver the finished, tested system along with documentation built to your standards, ready for you to present. If you want ongoing support, we offer hosting and maintenance so the systems keep running after launch.
Can you white-label the ongoing hosting and maintenance too?
Yes. We host and maintain the training systems and automations on a recurring plan under your brand, so your client relationship has a stable, recurring foundation instead of ending when the build ships.
What kinds of clients is this a fit for?
Growing teams that lean on operations, HR, and training, franchises, high-turnover businesses, nonprofits, and education organizations. If your client has SOPs and repeatable work, we can turn that into training and automation.
