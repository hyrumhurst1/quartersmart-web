Structure Properties: from handing out SOPs to a full AI training system

At Structure Properties, training used to run on memory and paperwork. When an employee made a mistake, they were handed the SOP and told to rewrite it by hand, like a middle-school punishment, so the lesson would stick. It did not scale. New hires slogged through dense documents, the same questions came back week after week, and owners had no real way to see who knew what.

We replaced that with a full AI-powered training and onboarding system: a 39-course video library across seven operational roles, AI-automated course and video creation, Claude-powered quizzes, automated certification, and an owner dashboard that shows who has completed what and where people struggle. The system is deployed on Vercel.

The result, across seven operational roles:

  • Onboarding time and repeat questions cut by 65 percent
  • SOP-related mistakes cut by 36 percent
  • Automated certification and compliance reporting, built in

Skytech: a voice agent that stopped the no-shows

The problem

Skytech was bleeding revenue to canceled, ghosted, and rescheduled appointments. Every empty slot was time and money gone, and the front office could not chase every booking by hand.

What we built

A voice agent paired with SMS appointment automation that confirms, reminds, and reschedules without a person on the phone for every call.

The result

  • Canceled, ghosted, and rescheduled appointments cut by 20 percent.

Fewer empty slots, less manual chasing, more appointments that actually happen.
